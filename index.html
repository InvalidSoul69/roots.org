About us

Vision Mission Values

Our Vision

We aspire to a Sudan where just peace prevails, where human rights are respected and justice flourishes in every corner, so that everyone can live in dignity and equality.

More

Our Mission

We seek to make a sustainable impact by promoting the values of human rights and democracy. We work to advocate for social justice issues and spread a culture of peace, while empowering individuals and communities to be leaders of positive change and participate in building a future based on equality and fairness.

More

Our Values

1. Social justice: Promoting equality of opportunity and individual rights.
2. Transparency: Commitment to clarity and credibility in all our activities.
3. Respect: Appreciating diversity and difference in society.
4. Human rights: Defending the rights of all individuals without discrimination.
5. Freedom: Empowering individuals and communities to express their opinions and needs.
