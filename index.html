Roots of Change for Building and Development Organization

The organization works to empower communities, develop youth capacities, and spread the values of peace and justice, to promote human rights and build a democratic future based on equality and respect for diversity and difference

  • Our vision: We aspire to a Sudan where just peace prevails, where human rights are respected and justice flourishes in every corner, so that everyone can live in dignity and equality.
  • Our mission: We seek to make a sustainable impact by promoting the values of human rights and democracy. We work to advocate for social justice issues and spread a culture of peace, while empowering individuals and communities to be leaders of positive change and participate in building a future based on equality and fairness.

Roots of Change for Building and Development Organization

We seek to enhance the capacity of communities and motivate youth to contribute effectively to making change, through collective action and commitment to our core values.
